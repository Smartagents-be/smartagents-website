This morning the coffee was ready for a room full of business owners. Together with **Aviso+** we welcomed their clients for a breakfast session around one question that lives in a lot of SMEs: how do you actually put AI to work in your business?

It is a question we hear often. AI sounds big, abstract, and a little far away. So we turned it around. No distant future, just tangible examples you can use tomorrow.

## AI doesn't have to be a distant promise

We don't like talking about AI as a magic box. They are **digital colleagues**. They take over repeatable work, strengthen your team, and make your processes lighter.

> "AI doesn't have to be a distant promise. They are digital colleagues that strengthen your team and make your work lighter."

## From demo to daily practice

That became tangible the moment we brought out the demos. One example: an **avatar that looks and sounds like you**, built from a single photo and a short piece of sample text. Useful for product videos, onboarding, training, and multilingual customer communication, without stepping in front of the camera every time.

Every example started from a recognisable task: drafting a quote, sorting a mailbox, summarising a file. The technology was never the point. The **work that gets lighter** was.

## The real impact is in the conversation

Thanks to **Aviso+** for the partnership and the hospitality. And thanks to everyone who joined, for the engaging conversations and the sharp questions. That is exactly where the real impact begins: not with the tool, but with the question of what it means for your business.
